# Examples — image attribution

The images in this folder are from the
[Circular Fashion v2](https://fnauman.github.io/second-hand-fashion/)
dataset, released under [CC-BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

Two single-image samples are redistributed here for the sole purpose of
letting users reproduce the measurement pipeline locally without
downloading the 32K-item dataset. Full credit and copyright remain with
the original authors.
